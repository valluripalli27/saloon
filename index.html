Salon & Spa Website App

Designed a responsive booking platform for salons and spas, focusing on streamlined appointment scheduling, service browsing, user trust, and payment integration.

🎯 Goal

To design a user-friendly salon booking experience optimized for all devices, enabling users to browse services, book appointments, view offers, and make secure payments in under 3 minutes.

🧑‍🎨 Role

Led the end-to-end UX/UI design from user research to wireframes, high-fidelity prototypes, and developer handoff.

👥 Team

Solo designer with collaboration from a backend developer and product owner. Responsible for research, interaction design, visuals, and testing.

📆 Timeframe

June — August 2023

🔍 Research & Insights

Methods: User surveys, competitor benchmarking, and usability testing

  • Speed matters: Users want to book quickly without signup barriers.
  • Trust is critical: Reviews, certifications, and stylist bios increase confidence.
  • Mobile-first behavior: 85% preferred booking via mobile.
  • Offers & loyalty: Many are motivated by discounts and loyalty perks.
💡 Design Strategy
  • Thumb-friendly UI, sticky “Book Now” buttons
  • Stripe/Apple Pay integration
  • Spa-inspired typography & layout
  • Real-time slots, timezone handling
  • Trust: bios, ratings, protocols
🧪 Wireframes & Flows
  • Home & landing pages
  • Service categories with filters
  • Stylist detail & ratings
  • Booking: Select → Time → Payment
  • Success & rebooking
🎨 High-Fidelity UI
  • Tools: Figma + Adobe XD
  • Responsive views: mobile, tablet, desktop
  • Atomic components
  • Pastel peach & deep lavender palette
  • Micro-interaction animations
💳 Payments & Services
  • Secure Stripe payments
  • Promo codes & tipping
  • Home visit toggle
  • Service add-ons
📈 Launch Results (Beta)
  • 40% faster bookings
  • Mobile engagement up 62%
  • 85% users rated 9+/10
  • Positive stylist feedback
📍 Landing Pages
  • Seasonal & event offers
  • Location-based service pages
  • Used in IG & Google Ads
🛠️ Next Steps
