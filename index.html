Salon & Spa Booking App

UX/UI Design • Responsive Web App • 10 Week Timeline

Project Overview

I designed and developed a full-featured salon and spa website with seamless navigation and a responsive user interface. The platform enables real-time service browsing, filtering, and appointment booking to ensure a smooth and intuitive user experience. Secure payments were integrated using Stripe and Apple Pay, along with features like optional tipping and a loyalty system. Additionally, I implemented location-based service filtering and customizable add-ons to personalize each booking.

Where UX Meets Self-Care: Designing a Frictionless Booking & Browsing Experience

I led the end-to-end UX design for BizInc’s new salon and spa web presence — a digital front door for walk-in customers and online bookings. The challenge? Create a seamless, visually inviting landing and service experience that felt as smooth as a head massage while integrating with real-time scheduling tools, service filters, and accessibility standards.

I owned the UX from initial research and wireframes to high-fidelity designs and developer handoff, collaborating with both product and engineering teams to ensure the site was responsive, fast, and brand-aligned across devices.

Team Size:5 Duration:7 months

Reception-First Design Approach

Designed the landing flow around first-time users making it feel like walking into a calm, welcoming lobby rather than navigating a cluttered menu.

Instant Service Discovery

Built category based browsing with filters for service types, stylist specialties, and locations like letting customers glance at a stylish menu, not dig through a drawer.

Booking Without the Wait

Created a one-step appointment flow that prioritized convenience no forced signups or long forms, just pick a service and time and you're done.

How Might We..

Designing a modern salon booking platform involves creating a seamless and trustworthy experience that enables users to schedule appointments quickly and effortlessly. Prioritizing mobile optimization ensures accessibility and convenience for users on the go, enhancing overall satisfaction.

Task Flow

Users can browse → choose a service → pick a time → pay — all in 3 clicks. Focus was placed on rebooking and sticky "Book Now" buttons.

Sketches & Wireframes

Started with low-fidelity hand-drawn sketches to ideate on structure and user flow. Converted these into digital wireframes for early testing.

Usability Testing

Tested flows with 5 users across mobile and desktop. Feedback helped refine the "select stylist" and "payment" steps. Simplified booking steps based on confusion points.

Visual Identity

Brand keywords: Calm, Elegant, Spa-like. Used soft peach and lavender tones with rounded components to match the spa aesthetic.

What I Learned

Real user research often reveals unexpected truths it helped challenge my assumptions. I also learned how valuable early sketching is: it’s cheaper to change paper than pixels. Designing with scalability in mind saved many hours during development handoff.
